# HydroGenAI

### AI-Enabled Smart Rooftop Rainwater Energy Harvesting System

HydroGenAI is an AI-enabled renewable energy system that harvests the energy of flowing rooftop rainwater and converts it into usable electrical power for residential buildings. The system combines micro-hydropower generation, battery storage, real-time IoT monitoring, and predictive energy analytics to provide an affordable, sustainable, and intelligent supplementary energy solution for regions with abundant rainfall.

Rainwater collected from rooftops is directed through gutters into a flow regulation chamber before passing through a nozzle that creates a high-velocity water jet. The jet drives a Pelton turbine coupled to a DC generator, producing electrical energy that is conditioned and stored in a rechargeable battery for later use.

HydroGenAI features an ESP32-based smart monitoring system that continuously measures key operating parameters such as voltage, current, power output, and battery status. Historical system data is analyzed using machine learning models to predict future energy generation based on previous rainfall events and system performance. These predictions help users anticipate available energy, optimize electricity usage, and improve overall energy management.

Designed for residential buildings in regions with moderate to high annual rainfall, HydroGenAI transforms an underutilized natural resource into clean electrical energy, enhancing household energy resilience while reducing dependence on conventional fossil-fuel-powered backup generators.
